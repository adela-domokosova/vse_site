About V$Epage

Welcome to our unique pixel art project inspired by the legendary "The Million Dollar Homepage", which was created exclusively for the community of students of Prague University of Economics and Business in Prague (VSE)!

Our website consists of a digital canvas with limited number of pixels, where you can buy each pixel for a symbolic price of 1 CZK per month. These pixels can then be colored to create colorful images, texts or patterns that will be displayed on our homepage. It is a space for students to showcase their creativity, and at the same time a competitive spirit among the VSE faculties will develop.

What's the catch? Each pixel purchase is temporary - after the end of the selected period, the funds will be donated to the students of the faculty with the most active teaching (tracked on our scoreboard). It's an interesting way to combine creativity, friendly rivalry and community support!

Are you ready to paint your own story? Make every pixel count!
